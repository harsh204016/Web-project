The ADR Portability Scale

Questions YES NO DON'T KNOW
1 Are there previous conclusive reports on the reactions?
2 Did the ADR appear after the suspected drug was administrative?
3 Did the ADR improve when the drug was dicontinued a specific atagonist was administered?
4 Did the adverse reaction reappear when the drug was re-administered?
5 Are there alternatives causes that could solely have caused the ADR?
6 Was the drug detected in the blood?(or other fluids)in a concentration known to be toxic?
7 Was the reaction more severe when the drug was increased,or less severe when the drug was decreased?
8 Did the patient have a similar reaction to the same or similar drug in any previous exposure?
9 Was the adverse event confirmed by the objective evidence?
